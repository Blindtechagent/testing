Features

Discover our collection of tools designed to make your daily tasks easier and more efficient. We’ve organized everything into categories so you can quickly find the right tool for the job.


  • Blind Tech Agent AI

    Chat with our smart AI to get answers to your questions, generate creative ideas, or simply have an engaging conversation.

  • BTA Image Generator

    Turn your ideas into images by simply describing what you want to see. Let the AI handle the creativity for you.

  • Age Calculator

    Find out exactly how old you are in years, months, and days. Just enter your birthday for an instant result.

  • Calculator

    Handle your daily math tasks with a straightforward calculator that’s easy to navigate and use.

  • Currency Converter

    Quickly convert between different global currencies using the latest exchange rates for travel or business.

  • Link Shortener

    Turn long, messy web links into short, clean URLs that are much easier to copy and share with others.

  • WhatsApp Link Generator

    Generate a link that lets people message you on WhatsApp immediately, without needing to save your number.

  • Telegram Link Generator

    Create direct links for your Telegram chat or group so others can connect with you with just one click.

  • Text Analyser

    Check the length and structure of your writing with accurate counts for characters, words, and lines.

  • Text Encrypter & Decrypter

    Keep your messages private by encoding them into secret code. You can easily decode them whenever you need.

  • Text to Image

    Convert your written thoughts or notes into styled images. Customize the look with different colors and fonts.

  • Text OCR

    Quickly extract text from images and photos. It’s a simple way to read printed materials using your screen reader.

  • Document Creator

    Create professional-looking documents and save them as Word, PDF, or text files while tracking your word count.

  • Media Player

    Listen to music or watch videos with a player built specifically for accessibility and ease of use.

  • Password Generator

    Generate strong, secure passwords to protect your accounts and keep them stored safely for when you need them.

  • Speaker Cleaner

    Use sound vibrations to help remove dust or moisture from your phone’s speakers and keep your audio clear.

  • Stop Watch

    Track your time for exercise, cooking, or any other activity with a simple and reliable stopwatch tool.

  • QR Generator

    Generate QR codes instantly for URLs, text, phone numbers, WhatsApp, and social media profiles.

  • To-Do List

    Stay on top of your day by listing your tasks and marking them as finished to keep yourself productive.

We hope these tools make your daily digital life a bit easier.
